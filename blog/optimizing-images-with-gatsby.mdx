# Optimizing Images with Gatsby 🖼️

Handling images efficiently is crucial for performance. Gatsby offers **gatsby-plugin-image**, a powerful image optimization tool.

## Benefits of Gatsby Image
- **Lazy Loading** 🚀 – Loads images only when needed.
- **Automatic Format Selection** 📷 – Uses WebP for better compression.
- **Blur-Up & Placeholder Effects** 🎨 – Improves perceived performance.
